Origami Crane(intermediate)



step 1. Put a square paper color side, then turn it 90 degrees to make a diamond shape

step 2. Fold in half to make a triangle and open, repeat for the other direction

step 3. Turn the paper over to the white side and fold it in half to make a rectangle, repeat for the other direction

step 4. Using the creases you have made, take the right and left corners into the middle and down to the bottom corner, then flatten

step 5. fold the left and right lower edges of the top layer towards the center crease, making a kite shape. Fold the top traingle down over the folded edges, creasing well. Unfold everything back until step 4.

step 6. Pull the bottom corner up, opening the top flap of the model. Bring it upwards, pressing the sides of the model at the same time. Some creases will become inverted. Flatten down and crease well.

step 7. Repeat steps 6 and 5 on the other side

step 8. Fold in the lower flaps of the top layer towards the center. Turn around and repeat on the other side.

step 9. Fold both legs of the model up. These will become the head and tail. Crease very well and unfold

step 10. Slightly open the side and bring the head part up. Bend the tip down using your finger, reverse the crease in the head. and pinch it to form the beak. Bring the tail up on the other

step 11. Last but not least, fold down the wings and pull them out a little bieng careful not to pull them to far or brake them(this will help inflate the back, if it is not inflating blow into the bottom). Now your origami crane is done!
