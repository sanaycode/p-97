Origami Pinwheel(easy-intermediate)



step 1. Fold the paper in half and then unfold it.

step 2. Fold both sides in to the center along the dotted lines.

step 3. Fold the top and bottom in to the center.

step 4. Unfold the folds from the previous step.

step 5. Open up the paper along the dotted lines.

step 6. Squash Fold the paper flat.

step 7. Open up the paper at the top and Squash Fold it flat.

step 8. Fold the paper out along the dotted lines.

step 9. Open up each of the 4 flaps.
